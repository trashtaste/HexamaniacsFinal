\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
implements functionality and acts as the compoiste participant 

Inherits from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classRocket}{Rocket}} class. It has a single rocket\+Cores member which is a vector that holds pointers to the Composite cores. Overides the adding, removing and executing methods defined by \mbox{\hyperlink{classRocket}{Rocket}}.
